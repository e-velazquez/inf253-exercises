Adaptogens

Welcome to my website about adaptogens! If you're wondering what an "adaptogen" is, this website give you a brief explanation. In addition, you will learn about the most common adaptogens found in our every day lives that do so much for our health and well being.

According to Merriam-Webster dictionary, an adaptogen is defined as: "a nontoxic substance and especially a plant extract that is held to increase the body's ability to resist the damaging effects of stress and promote or restore normal physiological functioning". My fascination with adaptogens began three years ago when I started working at a tea shop. A year later, I took a Theology course at Dominican University with Fr.Richard Woods, O.P. where the course revolved around spirituality and healing. Father Woods discussed holistic healing and how we can take advantage of adaptogens due to their powerful healing abilities. He lent me his book on adaptogens and I have not stopped doing my research on them since.

Common Adaptogens

Some of the most common adaptogens are:

  • Peppermint
  • Lavender
  • Ginger
  • Goji berry
  • Chamomile

Adaptogens are great because they help us fight daily stressors. They can easily be incorporated into our routines, as they can be consumed in different forms: raw, through teas, or in capsules. For the most part, there are very little side effects, as it is rare to overdose on adaptogens. They are also great because they are accessible and usually taste or smell great.
